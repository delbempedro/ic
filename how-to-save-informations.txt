- To save quantum computer info
    Acess https://quantum.ibm.com/composer
    Click on "Setup and Run" in up side right corner
    You will can see the available quantum computers
    Click on "See more details" in up side right corner of interest quantum computer box
    In down side right corner you can see a download icon
    Click in download icon and select your option (Download all calibration data (.csv); Download
    map view (.svg); Download map view (.png))
